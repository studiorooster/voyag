You Were Created for Fellowship

God is a God of relationships. He created us to fellowship with Him. He wants us to grow in the knowledge and understanding of that relationship. He desires close fellowship with us. So much that He invited us to call Him Abba Father – a phrase that literally means “Daddy”. God desires healthy earthly relationships for us too. To that end He created families, communities and friendships. His desire is for us to fully understand and embrace what all of that means. The greatest worship we can offer Him is to love Him with all of our heart, soul, mind and strength and to love others in His name.
