Founding Team

Patrick Middleton
President

Patrick Middleton is dedicated to helping people grow spiritually, mentally, physically, relationally and financially, so that everyone can realize their God-given potential. He holds a Master’s Degree in Counseling Psychology and a double-major Bachelor’s Degree in Psychology and Business Administration. For the last 14 years, he’s held a variety of roles in education, counseling, management, and sales, where exceptional client-centered relationships have been the key to his success. His hope, and prayer is that everyone enters into a genuine relationship with God through Jesus Christ, and God blesses them richly with the fruit of the spirit: love, joy, peace, forbearance, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness and self-control.

Dr. Bonnie Hedlund
Licensed Psychologist, & Advisory Board Member

Dr. Hedlund has dedicated most of her life towards the service of others. Being faithful to God’s calling for her life, she’s positively impacted countless lives as a licensed psychologist, college Professor, researcher, and church ministry leader. Dr. Hedlund holds a Ph.D in Psychology, a Master’s Degree in Experimental Psychology, a Master’s Degree in Ministry, and a Bachelor’s Degree in Psychology. She received her license as a Psychologist in 1991 and since then, has regularly participated in a variety of continuing education seminars with a special emphasis on neuropsychology and psychological assessment. Her current focuses include the training of new Christian therapists, integration of neurobiology and mental health, and helping to lift low socio-economic families out of poverty.

Larry Bevins
V.P. of Branding & Advisory Board Member

Larry is a dot connector. He began his career at Walt Disney, worked for a short time in radio and TV and went on to spend his life in advertising - launching new brands and re-positioning existing ones. His account experience includes companies like GE, Pfizer Pharmaceuticals, Ericsson, SONY, ETrade, KFC, Penn Racquet Sports and Purina. He has won over 100 awards in advertising, design and copy writing. He is the former principal and Creative Director at The Strata Group in Virginia and the former V.P. of Creative and Creative Director at The Townsend Agency in San Diego. He has also taught in the School of Art, Design and Art History at San Diego State University. He is presently the Principal and Creative Director at Brand360 in San Diego and is our V.P. of Branding and a Voyag Institute Advisory Board Member. He is a graduate of Parsons School of Design in Los Angeles. He is passionately dedicated to both the principles and the success of the Voyag Institute. Outside of work he can be found racing sports cars and is a Nationally Certified Performance Driving Instructor for Porsche Club of America.
