Overview

Our first community will be an online relational community based on Christian values made up of San Diego residents looking to grow in every aspect of their life spiritually, mentally, physically, relationally and financially, and interested in helping others do the same. The community will thrive through collaborative and professional efforts from San Diego based Pastors, Psychologists, Marriage and Family Therapists, Life Coaches, Physical Trainers, Nutritionists, Financial Planners and others that have dedicated themselves to helping people succeed in this life, and the next.
